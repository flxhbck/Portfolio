Hej, I’m Felix. I am a designer, technologist, and angry optimist with a passion for creating future interactions & products.

I work at Above in Malmö, where among other things, I help fusing technology and design with exploratory prototyping for projects that I mostly can't talk about (yet).

Sometimes I get to teach classes and workshops on tangible interfaces, interactive products, inflatables, and soon sustainability. Hyper Island interactive inflatables

At TEAGUE I helped design and prototype autonomous and airborne travel experiences. We examined how design can create trust in autonomous vehicles, used VR to prototype Car UX, and explored what crossing the street in the future of autonomous cars might feel like.

I also lead some efforts to explore the connected home space at TEAGUE Labs, where we created a lightbulb designed for graceful obsolescence, imagined novel ways to control IoT devices, and built an alarm clock that charges your phone.
In between projects we sometimes build somewhat goofier explorations, like reviving the Nintendo Power Glove for VR and putting googly eyes on VR googles.

I did my M.S. in Media Arts and Sciences at the Tangible Media Group at the MIT Media Lab where I got to explore novel materials uniMorph aeroMorph Pneuduino Sensory fiction and helped write a paper about hybrid interactions with phones o screens.

During my Bachelors in Digital Media at the University / University of the Arts Bremen, I learned the fundamentals of Computer Science, critical thinking, and design-oriented problem solving. With my Cuboino Wild&Tame Metadoors
